Shiny rates:

























Welcome to the shiny zone! Shiny pokemon are a rare specimen of pokemon in which the colour is different. Sometimes the change is subtle and other times it is very drastic. Every pokemon has a shiny, but what are the chances? Well, in pokemon go its a very very complicated question. So many events, factors and more can make a shiny as rare as 1/500 all the way down to 1/10. Using the left menu, you can learn more about each shiny rate in pokemon go, including pokemon that have them. The bottom navigation menu can also take you to more sites such as raid information, top counters for types etc. Please enjoy! :D
